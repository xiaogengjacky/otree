You and each member in your group will face {{ n }} decisions listed on each of your screens. On each screen, you will vote for your group's choices. {% blocktrans trimmed with hi=lottery_hi lo=lottery_lo %} On each screen "Option A" is a lottery that pays {{ hi }} with certain probabilities and {{ lo }} otherwise; "Option B" yields a varying payoff for sure, i.e. with a probability of 100%. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed with n=Constants.num_choices num_lists=Constants.num_rounds%} After you and your group members have made all of your votes for all screens, one of the screens (lists) will be randomly picked by the computer for payment. If your group, by a majority voting rule, voted to opt for "Option A" in this decision, option A will be chosen for your group, and it will be randomly determined (according to the specified probability) whether the low or high payoff in option A will constitute your and your group members' payoff; if your group, by a majority voting rule, voted to opt for "Option B", option B will be chosen your group, and the respective sure amount will constitute your and your group members' payoffs. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed with n=num_choices num_lists=Constants.num_rounds%} Even though you will make {{ n }} votes on each of the {{ num_lists }} screens, only one of the screen and one decision in that screen will end up affecting your and group members' earnings, but you will not know in advance which decision will be used.
