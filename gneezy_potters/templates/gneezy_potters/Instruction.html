In this experiment, you will make a series of investment decisions. In each round of the experiment, you will be given a budget and have an opportunity to invest. Both the rate of return and odd of success will be provided.

The earnings from the investment are calculated as follows: Investment Return = Return Rate * Investment Amount. Your total earning from a certain round will be: Total Earnings = Budget + Investment Return if the investment is successful; Total Earnings = Budget - Investment Amount if the investment fails.

At the end of the experiment, one round will be randomly chosen for payment and your total earnings from that round will be your final earnings for the experiment.
