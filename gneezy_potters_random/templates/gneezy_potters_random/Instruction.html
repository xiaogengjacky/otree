In this experiment, you will work with two other group members to make a series of investment decisions. In each round of the experiment, each of you will be given a same budget and have an opportunity to invest. Both the rate of return and odd of success will be provided.

Each of you will decide how much to invest, but the same investment amount will be applied to all three group members based on the following rule: one group member's decision will be randomly chosen and applies to all group members. You are member {{ within_id }} and the probability that your decision will be chosen is {{ weight }}.

The earnings, for each member, from the investment are calculated as follows: Investment Return = Return Rate * Investment Amount. Your total earning from a certain round will be: Total Earnings = Budget + Investment Return if the investment is successful; Total Earnings = Budget - Investment Amount if the investment fails.

At the end of the experiment, one round will be randomly chosen for payment and your total earnings from that round will be your final earnings for the experiment.
