{# Instructions#} 实验指南

在本实验中，你与另外一名参与者被随机分为了一组。你们每人代表一家公司。两个公司生产同样的产品。 {# You have been randomly and anonymously paired with another participant.#} {# Each of you will represent a firm. Both firms manufacture the same#} {# product.#}

你们二人依序决定自己的公司要生产多少单位的产品。其中一人将会首先决定。第二个决定的人在做决定前将会获知第一个人的决定。在做自己的 决定前你就会知道做决定的顺序。 {# The two of you decide sequentially how many units to manufacture.#} {# One of you will decide first. The other will be informed of the units#} {# produced by the first one and then decide the units for#} {# herself/himself.#} {# You will learn the order prior to making any decision.#}

你的决定可以是从0到{{ Constants.max_units_per_player }}间的任何数值。所有生产的产品都可以在市场上被售出，但是市场上的产品越多， 单位产品的售价越低。 {# Your choices can be any number from 0#} {# to {{ Constants.max_units_per_player }}.#} {# All produced units will be sold, but the more is produced, the lower#} {# the unit selling price will be.#}

你的利润为： {# Your profit is therefore:#}

  • 你的利润 = 单位产品售价×卖出的产品数量 {# Your profit = Unit selling price × Units produced by your#} {# firm#}
